PXN: Ghost Division
"In the Shadows of Web3"

The underbelly of Web3. A shadow vague, formless, but eternal." PXN: Ghost Division is an NFT collection of 10,000 anime-inspired warriors on the Ethereum blockchain. Each Ghost belongs to a regiment of 100 under a Phantom’s command, giving holders a place in this mysterious resistance.


The Spirit of PXN


PXN is not just an NFT collection, but a living, breathing community of rebels who thrive in the shadows of Web3. Each Ghost carries unique traits and stories, shaping the culture of their regiment and strengthening the resistance. With a strong emphasis on art, utility, and identity, PXN provides holders with more than just digital collectibles — it offers a place in a growing movement. From exclusive access to tools and collaborations, to the power of the $PXN token, every Ghost becomes part of something larger than themselves. In PXN, individuality meets unity, creating a brand that evolves through the voices of its holders.
